Privacy Policy (How is my data used?)

The access that you provide to this application is used only to subscribe your Google account to an additional calendar. This application uses the authentication you provide to access the Google API on your behalf.

This application does not retrieve your personal data, information from your Google account, or any details about your calendars or other information you may provide it access to, the only function of this application is to add a subscription.

This is a static website with no server side application component. Once you have loaded the webpage, at all times, your data is not kept anywhere other than the computer/device you are using and your Google account.

This application does not store any data in the form of cookies or other local storage on your device. Once you have navigated your browser away from this page, the application will not retain information about your session and will lose the ability to act on your behalf.

Google and Google Calendar are a third party and may store cookies and/or other information both at their end and on your device. For more information, you should refer to their privacy policy as appropriate.
